Short Bio

Seasoned Instructional Designer, Developer, and Enablement Specialist with 6.5 years of experience in crafting eLearning solutions. My expertise extends to developing accessible learning materials, managing large-scale training programs, and utilizing methodologies such as ADDIE and AGILE. I also have experience in graphic design and technical documentation, with proficiency in Articulate 360, Adobe Creative Suite, Salesforce, and LMS tooling. I am known for my strong communication skills and openness to new challenges in the learning field.
